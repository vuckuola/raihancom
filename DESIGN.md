## Overview

Raihan Com should feel like a credible local trade desk for office asset disposal: direct, calm, premium, and practical. The design borrows from Apple’s object-led restraint and showroom-like pacing, but keeps the Indonesian contact-first business reality visible without exposing private numbers.

## Colors

- **Ink / Charcoal:** high-trust surfaces and CTA contrast.
- **Paper / Porcelain:** warm, non-sterile background for local business credibility.
- **Brass:** value, trade, and premium cue; never overused.
- **Trade Blue:** action and reliability accent.

## Typography

Large compressed headlines make the site sell quickly. Body copy stays human, short, and operational.

## Layout

Hero is object-led: accepted office assets are visually shown as valuable physical inventory, not generic icons. Sections use generous breathing room and clear category blocks.

## Elevation & Depth

Use cinematic product shadow and soft card depth. Avoid neon gradients, cartoon illustrations, and fake dashboard visuals.

## Shapes

Cards can be softly rounded; product image containers should feel like clean studio/catalog trays. Controls use pill shapes for WhatsApp actions.

## Components

Primary CTA is contact/detail submission. Asset cards must always pair a clear label with a concrete object/category.

## Do's and Don'ts

Do:
- Show office equipment as real tangible objects.
- Keep claims conservative and trustworthy.
- Make contacts visible above the fold.
- Use static performance-first HTML/CSS.

Don't:
- Use fake testimonials, fake stats, or fake client logos.
- Use abstract AI blobs for equipment.
- Expose private phone numbers in template mode.
- Overdecorate with generic SaaS gradients.
